Here are some quick notes about effectively utilizing our website.


The Reservation App..


* This app is present on the respective pages of each of the three major dining spots at Mason. The links to each page is available on the navigaton bar.
* We simply click on the "BOOK A TABLE" button to access the reservation page. The Button looks like the following

* Once on the reservation page we can enter the date, time and the suitable table along with our contact information and hit submit.
This accepts the request for your reservation and you will be called to confirm your reservation as soon as possible.
* Cancellation of reservation is just a Phone call away. The numbers are listed below on this page and the homepage for the restaurants.


The Feedback App..


* Your Feedback is very valuable to us. So we have added the feedback on the navigation bar.
So that it can be accessed easily at any time on any point of the website.
* The form is simple enter your name, email and your comments, then chose a rating for our website and hit submit.
* Please connect with us using the popular social media options available easily at the bottom navigation bar
to show your support and to keep in touch with your friends at Mason Dining.
